Cheeseburger Recipe

Description:

This burger isn't for the faint of heart. 15 layers of cheese with ketchup and mustard, all between two warm sesame buns. Putting the CHEESE in Cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • 15 - Slices of Cheese (Any kind)
  • Ketchup and Mustard to taste.
  • 1 - Sesame Burger Bun.

Steps:

  1. Slice your bun in half.
  2. Toast both halves of the bun.
  3. Pile all 15 slices of cheese onto the bottom bun.
  4. Squeeze Ketchup and Mustard on the top bun to taste.
  5. Put the top bun on the cheese, and serve.
