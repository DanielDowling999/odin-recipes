Salad Recipe

Description:

This burger isn't for the faint of heart. Because it's not a burger. It's a salad, made from just lettuce and croutons.

Ingredients

  • 1 - Whole Head of Lettuce
  • Leftover Bread or Premade Croutons
  • 1/4 Cup - Olive Oil
  • Seasoning to Taste

Steps:

  1. Chop head of lettuce into desired leaf size.
  2. If croutons are pre-made skip the following steps:
  3. Dice your leftover bread into the desired size and toss in olive oil.
  4. Heat oven to 190C (375F), spread bread cubes in a single layer on a tray.
  5. Bake till golden, turning once halfway through. Cooking time depends on the cube thickness, but will generally take 15-20 mins
  6. Let croutons cool completely.
  7. Mix lettuce and croutons in a bowl, and serve.
